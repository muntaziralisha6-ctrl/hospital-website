About Aetheris Hospital

Excellence in healthcare since 1998

Our Story — Healing Beyond Boundaries

Aetheris Hospital was founded in 1998 with a mission to bring world-class medical care to every individual. What started as a 50-bed facility has grown into a 500+ bed multi-specialty tertiary care hospital, recognized globally for clinical excellence.

Over 25+ years, we have treated over 500,000 patients, performed groundbreaking surgeries, and pioneered minimally invasive techniques. Our commitment to research and compassion makes us a trusted name in modern medicine.

Today, we are proud to be JCI-accredited and ranked among the top hospitals for patient safety and outcomes.
